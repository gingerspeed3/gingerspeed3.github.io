    Join Team 2/5 RSM

    WE ARE LOOKING FOR A FEW GOOD MEN ... AND WOMEN

    Friends of the 2/5, we are building an organization that will fulfill the goal of providing lasting support and assistance to the 2/5 Marines and their families. If you feel like we do, that by adopting the 2/5 we committed to provide a sustained level of support to those who give so much, and if you are as inspired and passionate about our Marines as we are, then you will want to join Team 2/5 RSM and get to work!

    We'll be establishing a regular meeting schedule for Team 2/5 RSM and organizing ad hoc committees for Team 2/5 RSM projects and events. If you're interested in joining Team 2/5 RSM, fill out the Contact Information form below.
